The Bear River Camp Trails offer approximately 3 to 5 miles of multi-use paths at the site of the former Bear River Campground near Colfax. The trail network hugs the river's edge along its gentler sections before winding up the canyon slopes for more challenging terrain.

Easier riverside paths are accessible and scenic, following the natural contours of the Bear River. The uphill segments require more effort but reward with canyon views and a greater sense of wilderness.

The trails are open to hikers, mountain bikers, and leashed dogs. Whether you're looking for a mellow riverside stroll or a workout on the canyon slopes, Bear River Camp has options for a range of fitness levels.
