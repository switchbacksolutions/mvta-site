The Heather Glen Trail climbs Hotchkiss Hill for one of the area's most rewarding short hikes. In just one mile, the route gains roughly 500 feet to a summit that opens up sweeping panoramic views: the Sierra Nevada mountains stretch to the east while the Sacramento Valley spreads westward on clear days.

The trail is accessible through Heather Glen Estates off Applegate Road and is open to hikers, cyclists, equestrians, and leashed dogs. Because the path passes close to private residences, users are asked to keep noise to a minimum and respect the neighborhood.

With its compact distance and spectacular views, this is a favorite for early risers and sunset seekers alike.
