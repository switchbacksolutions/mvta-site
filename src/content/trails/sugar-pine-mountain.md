The Sugar Pine Mountain Trail is a dedicated single-track loop that circles the Winchester development while climbing to the summit of Sugar Pine Mountain, offering some of the best valley views in the Meadow Vista area. On a clear day, Mount Diablo is visible to the west from the peak.

The 6.3-mile route features pleasant woodland walking on firm-packed dirt with generally good footing throughout. Multiple creek crossings on wooden footbridges add charm to the lower sections. The trail's biggest challenge is a steep 500-foot climb with switchbacks across Sugar Pine Ridge — a worthy effort for the panoramic payoff at the top.

The trail is open to hikers, mountain bikers, equestrians, and leashed dogs. Because the route passes close to private residences near the Winchester development, users must stay on the marked path at all times.
