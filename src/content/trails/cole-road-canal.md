The Cole Road Bear River Canal Loop is a convenient 3.6-mile loop that combines safe roadside walking along Cole Road with a dedicated trail section following the Bear River Canal. The route is one of the area's most accessible outings, with gentle terrain suitable for hikers and leashed dogs.

The canal trail portion provides a pleasant, semi-shaded path alongside the historic waterway before the loop returns via Cole Road. Because the route passes through private property, users are asked to keep dogs on leash and to respect the surrounding landowners at all times.

This loop makes a great morning or evening walk for residents and visitors looking for a low-key outing in the Meadow Vista area.
