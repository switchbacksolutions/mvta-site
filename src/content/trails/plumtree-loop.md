The Plumtree Road – Bear River Canal Loop is a pleasant 4.8-mile route that blends canal utility road, canal trail, and hiking paths into a satisfying loop above the Bear River Canal near Meadow Vista.

The loop begins at the Plumtree utility road turnout and follows the utility road above the canal before descending via a steep hiking path. Hikers cross a green steel bridge over the canal, pass through an old group campground area, then continue along the canal trail. The route eventually crosses Plumtree Road and parallels the Bear River Canal trail back to the start.

Open to hikers, mountain bikers, and leashed dogs, this loop is a great option for a midweek outing or an easy weekend adventure with the family pet.
