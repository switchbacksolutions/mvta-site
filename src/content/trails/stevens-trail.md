The Stevens Trail is one of the most scenic and historically significant trails in our area. Originally built as a toll road for pack mules traveling between Colfax and the gold fields of Iowa Hill, the trail now offers hikers, mountain bikers, and equestrians a memorable journey into the North Fork American River canyon.

The route begins in Colfax and gradually descends through chaparral and oak woodland, revealing stunning canyon views and seasonal waterfalls along the way. The return climb is the true challenge — carry plenty of water and plan to avoid the midday heat, as heat exhaustion is a common issue.

**Multi-use courtesy reminder:** Mountain bikers and hikers are asked to use extreme caution on blind corners in the narrow sections of this trail to avoid startling horses.
