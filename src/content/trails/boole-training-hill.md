Boole Training Hill is exactly what its name suggests: a demanding climb that rewards the effort with canyon views at multiple points along the ascent. Located off Cerro Vista Road in the Applegate area, this steep out-and-back is a favorite for trail runners and fitness hikers looking for a solid elevation challenge.

The route offers several natural rest spots where you can pause and take in views of the surrounding canyon. The trail is open to hikers, mountain bikers, equestrians, and leashed dogs, though the descent is steep enough that equestrians should lead their horses on the way down.

Come prepared with plenty of water for this one — the climb is relentless and the exposure can be significant on warm days.
