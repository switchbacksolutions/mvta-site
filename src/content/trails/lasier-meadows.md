Little Lasier Meadows Horse Camp is a dedicated equestrian facility that serves as a gateway to some of the most expansive trail networks in the Sierra Nevada. Situated in the high country, the camp provides direct access to trails within both the Tahoe National Forest and the Eldorado National Forest.

From camp, riders can connect to the Pacific Crest Trail, explore routes around Jackson Meadows Reservoir, or head toward the American River Trail for multi-day adventures. Horse paddocks and an arena are available on site.

Whether you're planning a weekend ride or a longer backcountry trip, Little Lasier Meadows is an ideal staging point for equestrians looking to explore hundreds of miles of national forest trails.
