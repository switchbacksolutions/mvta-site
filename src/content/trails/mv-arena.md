The MV Arena Trail system connects the Meadow Vista area's main road corridors via a network of single-track paths with easy grades and generally even, firm-packed footing. The routes are well suited to hikers, cyclists, equestrians, and leashed dogs.

From the trailhead at Placer Hills and Combie Roads, you can follow Combie Road north approximately 2.5 miles to the Meadow Vista Riding Arena, where ample parking is available for horse trailers. An alternative route heads north along Placer Hills Road past Walgra Meadows Road to a covered bridge at the Simpson Ranch property.

Be aware that poison oak grows along some trail sections, and no water is available on the trail — carry what you need. The trail is best enjoyed outside of the rainy season (mid-November through mid-April) when the paths can become muddy.
