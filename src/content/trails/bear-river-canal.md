The Bear River Canal Trail between Placer Hills Road and Meadow Gate Road is one of the area's most accessible routes, offering flat, semi-shaded miles along a historic canal corridor with documented use dating back to the 1940s.

The trail connects two road access points, making it a pleasant out-and-back for hikers, cyclists, and families. The gentle grade and even terrain make it a great choice for users of all abilities.

Because the route passes through private property, users are asked to keep dogs on leash at all times and to respect the surrounding landowners with quiet, considerate behavior. Cyclists should slow and yield when passing other trail users on narrow sections.
