Skillman Campground is the trailhead for a delightful network of forest paths weaving through pine and oak woodland near Nevada City. The trail system includes the Pioneer and Hallelujah trails, along with singletrack, two-track paths, and Forest Service roads that can be combined into loops or multi-day rides.

Creek crossings add character to the routes, and the varied terrain suits hikers, mountain bikers, and equestrians alike. OHV use is permitted on designated sections.

The campground itself offers seven equestrian sites, each with a picnic table, fire ring, bear bin, and corral, plus vault toilets and water troughs. The facility is open May through September. Note that the water spigots are not activated — bring all your own drinking water. Cell service is unreliable in the area, so download your maps before heading out.
