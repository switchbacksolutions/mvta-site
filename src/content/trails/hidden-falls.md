Hidden Falls Regional Park is one of the most beloved outdoor destinations in Placer County, offering trails that wind through open grasslands, riparian creeksides, and oak-forested hillsides — all within easy reach of Meadow Vista.

The park's multi-use trails are suitable for hikers, mountain bikers, and equestrians. A wheelchair-accessible paved pathway near the parking area loops around a nearby hill, making the park welcoming to visitors of all abilities. For those venturing deeper into the park, waterfalls and cascades are visible from dedicated viewing decks along the creek.

The equestrian trailhead is located at the far end of the main parking lot. Multiple trail options allow visitors to customize the length and challenge of their outing.
