The Walgra Meadows and Combie Road Loop is a convenient 2.5-mile neighborhood loop starting from the Holiday Market in Meadow Vista. The route follows the Combie Road trail to a utility road leading through Walgra Meadows, then returns along Placer Hills Road.

The loop offers easy, accessible walking with pastoral scenery through the meadows and along the canal. With minimal elevation change and a compact distance, it's ideal for a quick outing before or after errands, or as an easy introduction to the local trail network.

Multiple access points exist along the route, so the loop can be started from various locations. Use caution near the beginning of the route where the trail runs close to road traffic, and keep dogs on leash throughout.
